Why Air-Sealed Freshness?

Nuts and seeds naturally contain oils that can oxidize when exposed to air, affecting both freshness and flavor. Each serving of Dad's Breakfast granola is individually air-sealed to protect against oxidation — preserving the taste and quality of every batch.

Individual packs also make busy mornings easier. They're convenient on the go, help with portion control, and ensure that every serving is as fresh as the first.
