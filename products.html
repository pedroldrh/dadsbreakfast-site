Frequently Asked Questions

How long does shipping take?

Orders typically ship within 1-2 business days. Standard delivery takes 3-5 business days depending on your location. You'll receive a tracking number once your order ships.

What is the shelf life?

Thanks to our air-sealed packaging, each serving stays fresh for up to 3 months from the date of production. Once opened, we recommend consuming within 5-7 days for the best flavor and crunch.

Does this contain any common allergens?

Our granola contains tree nuts (almonds, cashews, coconut). It is free of wheat, gluten, dairy, eggs, soy, and peanuts. All products are made in a kitchen that handles tree nuts. Please check individual packaging for the most up-to-date allergen information.

Is local pickup available?

Yes! We offer free local pickup in Lexington, VA. You can also find us on campus at the Coop. Email us at dadsbreakfastgranola@gmail.com to arrange a pickup time.

Can I place a bulk or wholesale order?

Absolutely! We'd love to work with you on bulk orders for events, offices, or retail. Please reach out via our contact page or email us directly with the quantity you need and we'll send you a custom quote.

What makes this granola different?

Every serving is handmade in small batches using whole, recognizable ingredients — no grains, no refined sugar, no artificial additives. Each portion is individually air-sealed to prevent oxidation and preserve freshness, so the last serving tastes just as good as the first.
